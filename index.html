Who We Are

Much more than dancers

Founded in 2009, Ramblin' Raas competes across the nation in an Indian dance style known as Garba-Raas. We've traveled all over the country together, and we can't wait to keep growing. The Raas circuit is incredibly strong, with over 11 bid competitions, numerous other non-bid competitions, and a national championship: Raas All-Stars.

We dance together. We study together. We eat together. Some of us even live together. We watch raas videos, tag each other in memes, party, chill, watch movies together, play games, make glow in the dark puzzles, fly balsa wood airplanes, you name it. In other words, the team is more than just a team ‚Äî we're a family.
